Jeff Bezos' Blue Origin enters the space data center game

TechCrunch | March 20, 2026

Blue Origin has filed with the FCC for 'Project Sunrise,' a network of over 50,000 satellites that would serve as orbital data centers. The proposal aims to shift energy and water-intensive computing away from terrestrial facilities. SpaceX, Starcloud, and Google are all pursuing similar space data center concepts, betting on falling launch costs from SpaceX's Starship rocket.

TechCrunch | March 20, 2026
